Our Story

As a computer engineering student in college, I was no stranger to stress and anxiety. It wasn't until I reached out for help that I realized how difficult it can be to find the right counselor and resources to address my mental health concerns. It was then that I realized that there needed to be a platform that made it easy for people to connect with professional counselors who could help them address their mental and emotional well-being. That's why I created CounselMate, a safe and confidential space where anyone can connect with professional counselors who specialize in the areas they need help with. Our goal is to make it easy and accessible for everyone to get the help they need to improve their mental and emotional well-being. We believe that everyone deserves the chance to lead a happy, healthy, and fulfilling life, and we're here to help you do just that.
